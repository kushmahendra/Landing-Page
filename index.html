The Transfer of Real Estate

The transfer of real estate involves the legal process of changing ownership from one party to another. This process typically includes a series of steps such as conducting title searches, drafting and signing transfer documents, and recording the transaction with the appropriate government office. The transfer ensures that the new owner has clear and legal rights to the property, facilitating the seamless transition of property ownership.
